The Land of Smiles

The Thailand Hub

Spiritual temples, turquoise waters, and vibrant city life.

Bangkok

Hotels to Stay

  • Mandarin Oriental: A historic riverside gem with unparalleled service.
  • The Siam: A boutique Art Deco masterpiece.

Favorite Temples

  • Wat Arun: The stunning "Temple of Dawn" on the river.
  • Wat Phra Kaew: Home to the Emerald Buddha.

Phuket

Hotels to Stay

  • Amanpuri: The gold standard of luxury pavilions.
  • Rosewood Phuket: A sustainable paradise at Emerald Bay.

Favorite Temples

  • Wat Chalong: The largest and most revered temple on the island.
  • Big Buddha: 45 meters of white marble with 360-degree views.

Chiang Mai & Rai

Hotels to Stay

  • Raya Heritage: Inspired by northern Thai craft and culture.
  • Four Seasons Tented Camp: A luxury escape in the Golden Triangle.

Favorite Temples

  • Wat Rong Khun (White Temple): A contemporary masterpiece in Chiang Rai.
  • Wat Phra That Doi Suthep: A sacred site overlooking Chiang Mai.

Koh Samui

Hotels to Stay

  • Six Senses Samui: Incredible ocean-view villas.
  • W Koh Samui: Modern, chic, and high-energy.

Favorite Temples

  • Wat Plai Laem: Known for the 18-arm statue of Guanyin.
  • Wat Phra Yai: The Big Buddha Temple on the northern coast.

Pattaya

Hotels to Stay

  • Mason Pattaya: Ultra-modern beachfront villas.
  • Hilton Pattaya: Stunning views overlooking the bay.

Favorite Sights

  • Sanctuary of Truth: A hand-carved teak architectural wonder.
  • Wat Phra Yai: Big Buddha Hill for panoramic city views.
